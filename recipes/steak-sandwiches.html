Steak Sandwiches!

Description

These great steak sandwiches are one of my favorites to make. The tender asada steak is one of my favorites to use but you can use any seasoning you prefer on a thin tender steak. Allowing this dish to be very versatile. The great ciabatta bun holds all flavors and top with veggies of choice and a great sauce and be ready for this steak sandwich to fulfill your greatest sandwich desires.

Ingredients

  • Carne Asada steak (I get mine at a local market but can be made with a thin cut steak and seasonings of preference)
  • 2-Ciabatta Buns
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • Avocado
  • tomato
  • Spinach
  • Mayo
  • Sriracha

Steps

  1. Heat oiled cast iron skillet or grill to medium high
  2. While pan heats prep veggies, slice onion and bell pepper into strips. Slice tomato and either mash avocado or slice it.
  3. Once pan is heated and cook onion and bell pepper until softened and slightly translucent and remove from pan
  4. Cook steak in same pan until medium rare, try to get a nice sear on the steak maybe increase temp if needed.
  5. While steak cooks slice and toast ciabatta on grill or in oven for 3-5 min
  6. Once steak is done remove and let rest while you mix mayo and sriracha together in bowl
  7. Slice steak in to smaller strips if desired
  8. Assemble sandwiches, Start by spreading mayo sriracha mix on both sides of bun and then add steak to the bottom bun top with spinach and other veggies finally add avocado to top bun and close.
  9. Enjoy with chips or fries!!!
