Buffalo Chicken Mac N Cheese

Description

This Buffalo Mac and Cheese is a Gangsta Chef classic loved by all! It can be made with any cheese desired but I prefer a sharp cheddar and pepper jack. You'll be using cheese everywhere with a combination of a cheese sauce and layering cheese throughout the dish, for a the ultimate mac and cheese. In combination with cheesy richness you'll be adding crunchy spicy buffalo chicken for that added awesomeness! Perfect for party's, dinner or the hell of it!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of preferred dried pasta (I use elbow and shells)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 2 1/2 cups half and half
  • 4 cups shredded sharp cheddar
  • 2 cups pepper jack
  • 1/2 tbsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 6-8 breaded chicken tenders
  • Favorite buffalo sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 and grease a 9x13" baking dish
  2. Bring large pot of salted water to boil. When boiling add dried pasta and cook to al dente. 8-10 min. Drain and drizzle with a little bit of olive oil when done.
  3. While water is coming to boil shred both cheeses.
  4. Melt butter in large saucepan over medium heat. Once melted slowly incorporate flour while whisking to combine. Cook for about 1 minute continuing to whisk.
  5. Start slowly adding in about 2 cups of milk and half and half while whisking constantly until mixture is smooth. Slowly add remaining milk and half and half.
  6. Continue to heat over medium heat, whisking often until thickened (should be very thick like condensed soup)
  7. While sauce thickens add chicken strips to air fryer at 400 degrees for 12 min tossing halfway through
  8. Remove sauce from heat once thickened and add spices and 1 1/2 cups of shredded cheeses. Stir until melted them stir in another 1 1/2 cups of cheeses and stir until smooth.
  9. In a large bowl mix drained pasta with cheese sauce. Mix until all pasta is nice and cheesy. Then add half of the cheesy pasta to baking dish spreading throughout pan. Add a layer of shredded cheese.
  10. Once chicken tenders are done remove from air fryer cut into chunks and toss with buffalo sauce and add to baking dish.
  11. Add remaining cheesy pasta covering chicken and shredded cheese and top with remaining shredded cheese. Bake on 350 for 15-18 min until warm and top cheese is melted. (Can broil for a few min if you want top cheese crunchy.)
  12. Serve and Enjoy! Top with additional buffalo sauce for more kick!
