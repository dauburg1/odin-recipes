Kielbasa and Potatoes!

Description

Delicious and easy Kielbasa and Potato Skillet! This dish is easy and affordable while packing amazing flavor and spice. The hatch green chilli makes this dish amazing!

Ingredients

  • 1 Kielbasa link
  • 3-5 Russet Potatoes
  • Desired amount of Hatch green chilli
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 white or yellow onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Any other vegetables you would like can be added (green beans, corn, etc.)

Steps

  1. bake potatoes in microwave for 10 min until once done refrigerate while prepping
  2. dice onion and bell pepper into desired size
  3. mince garlic
  4. Chop chilli
  5. Heat enough oil to have about 1/8" to 1/4" in large cast iron skillet over medium high heat
  6. While oil heats dice baked potatoes into 1/2" pieces
  7. Add potatoes to heated oil and fry
  8. While potatoes cook split kielbasa link in half and cut into 1/4" thick pieces
  9. Once potatoes are golden brown and starting to get crispy add onion and bell pepper and cook until softened
  10. Once onion and peppers are softened add garlic, chilli and kielbasa
  11. Cook until potatoes are crispy outside and soft inside and kielbasa has light char marks
  12. Remove from heat and transfer to plate lined with paper towels if there is to much excess oil
  13. Serve with more green chilli if desired. (Ketchup makes a great topper or a little sour cream and salsa.)
